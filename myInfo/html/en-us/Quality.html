Prokit’s is an ISO-9001 registered manufacturer since 1998, all products’ meet or exceed quality standards and quality control procedures conform to the strict requirements of the standard.

Quality is the foundation of the Pro’sKit brand. To enhance capability in quality control and reliability testing for providing consistent quality product, Prokit’s has been investing in test equipment and has established a quality assurance laboratory since 2007. It has also built up computerized Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) for its production line.

Most of electronic power products are qualified to CE, GS, UL or TUV international standards and has been devoted to conforming to RoHS since 2006 per the European Union’s regulation. Quality is Prokit’s permanent promise, always secures high product quality for its customers throughout the world.
